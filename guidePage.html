PHASMAPHOBIA

Secrets and Easter eggs

by Author on May 01, 2022 at 09:25 AM

In the Phasmophobia ghost hunter simulator from Kinetic Games, you are invited to explore otherworldly forces, while trying not to become their victim. Themed Easter eggs, referring to famous works of this genre, fit perfectly into the atmosphere of the horror. Let’s talk about them.

Prison jokes

The prison is one of the most extensive locations in the game, often discouraging new players with its long corridors, in which there is practically no place to hide from a Ghost. There are two floors and two separate prison blocks, and therefore many pass by interesting little things — the map is too big to pay enough attention to them at the same time and not get lost.

Judging by the Easter eggs that can be found here, the developers clearly like jokes with wordplay. Before entering the prison for the first time, pay attention to the name plate above the door: it says «N.E. More Penitentiary», which sounds just like «Anymore Penitentiary» (you can try to translate as «even more corrections»). However, it is not known whether this is a reference to something specific, or whether the developers just found it funny.

Bloody Mary, Bloody Mary, Bloody Mary

There is an old urban legend according to which you can summon a ghost if you say «bloody Mary» three times while standing in front of a mirror in a dark room. In different variations of this legend, the ritual of summoning may differ, but one detail — to look in the mirror and pronounce the name three times — remains unchanged.

This horror story has firmly established itself in Western culture, becoming an attribute of children’s and teenage sleepovers, so it’s not surprising that the developers of Phasmophobia decided to add a reference to it to the game. Here, the ability to recognize the player’s voice available in the game came in handy.

666, The Beat's record

The number 666, called the number of the beast in the Bible, has become widespread in popular culture as an attribute of something supernatural, sinister, diabolical. There is even a song about him, «The Number of the Beast» by Iron Maiden. It is not surprising that in the game about paranormal activity there was an Easter egg associated with these three figures.

In the starting area, players can pass the time before loading the next game by practicing with a basketball hoop. Right above it hangs a counter that allows you to track your progress, and it is here that the Easter egg lurks. As soon as the 666th ball flies into the ring, thunder will thunder, and if you were not ready for it, the sound can even make you flinch from surprise.
