GAMING GARWARE

Review of the Acer OMW125 gaming Mouse

by Author on May 01, 2022 at 09:25 AM

In early April, the Taiwanese company Acer introduced an update to its line of gaming accessories with ten devices at once. These included the inexpensive Acer OMW125 mouse.

Acer OMW125 is a gaming six—button mouse with a sensor resolution of 3200 DPI, unregulated 7-color backlight and an affordable price of 1,390 rubles. I will tell you more about the model below.

Characteristics:

Sensor: Optical
Maximum resolution: 3,200 DPI
Number of buttons: 6
Backlight: yes, 7-color
Backlight mode: Flicker
Average working out: 5 million clicks
Connection: Wired, USB
Cable length: 1.8 meters
Dimensions: 127*73*39 mm
Weight: 146 grams

Appearance

The Acer OM W 125 mouse comes in a simple dark blue cardboard box with a transparent window on the front side, allowing you to see the manipulator without removing it from the package. The back side lists the characteristics of the model and technical information.

The mouse is a symmetrical device of a fairly large size and with a solid weight. It is completely made of black plastic, the upper part is smooth. There are no rubberized elements or soft-touch coatings, but this does not cause any problems with ergonomics. The plastic is of good quality, with active use there are no traces of exploitation in the form of prints or any scuffs on it.

On the left side there are auxiliary buttons designed for use in games. They can also be useful when working in a browser where they are assigned the action of going back or forward to the page. In other situations, they do not perform any actions, the ability to change their functions is not provided. The volume of pressing all buttons is average.

The connection cable has a length of 1.8 meters. It is braided, the connection interface is USB.

In general, the mouse has an ergonomic shape, the buttons are located well. The thumb fits exactly on the side keys, there is no need to look for them. The device slides well on the table and rug, has an unobtrusive backlight.

The only drawback that I was able to identify is the creaking of the case during operation.

The Good:

+ Low price
+ High-quality assembly
+ Unobtrusive design
+ Ergonomic housing
+ Long braided cable

The Bad:

- The case creaks when used
- Immutable backlight

9
